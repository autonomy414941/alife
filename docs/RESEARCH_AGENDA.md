# Research Agenda

## Current Direction
Over the next month, decompose whether the current founder-protection gains are upstream species-generation wins or merely downstream clade-structuring inheritance from realized species histories. Prioritize evaluation-surface honesty: add a second null family that does not condition on realized species, shrink the study/runtime surfaces that evaluation work reopens, and face the strongest anti-evidence before attempting more mechanism knobs. If decomposition reveals the gain is mostly species-level, the system needs richer interaction structure or phenotypic diversity before clade-level interventions can matter.

## Why This Direction
The March 15, 2026 founder-crowding validation is now in code, tests, and artifacts, and founder grace still improves the static-habitat baseline under the stricter habitat-plus-crowding null (`+9` active-clade delta improvement at cladogenesis threshold `1.0`, `+4` at `1.2`). That means the immediate blocker is no longer "does one more founder-context control erase the gain?" The blocker is that every tested variant still loses to its null in absolute active clades (`-25.75` for founder grace under habitat-plus-crowding matching; best current ecology gate `-17` with a large persistent-activity regression), while the matched null still repartitions realized species histories and therefore cannot distinguish upstream innovation from clade relabeling. Recent refactors already extracted study-result builders, relabel-null runners, and evolution-history helpers, so the highest leverage now is cleaner evaluation plus the next targeted splits.

## Structural Constraints
`buildMatchedSchedulePseudoClades()` still constructs pseudo-clades by repartitioning realized `species` histories, so any intervention that improves species origination timing, abundance, or persistence is partly inherited by the null. `src/activity.ts` (1860 lines), `src/simulation.ts` (2192 lines), `src/clade-activity-relabel-null-new-clade-establishment-horizon-study.ts` (689 lines), and `src/types.ts` (1223 lines) still concentrate too many responsibilities, so each evaluation change reopens the same large files. The simulator also still accumulates full `TaxonHistory.timeline` histories and per-tick `localityFrames`, which keeps long-horizon validation expensive and raises the cost of any future counterfactual or genealogy work.

## Revision History
- 2026-03-15 (PM): Non-species-conditioned null implemented (a70776b) and abundance-weighted metrics landed (662767d), removing the immediate evaluation-surface blockers. Recent coexistence theory (Theoretical Population Biology 2025) shows mechanism combinations extend coexistence more than tenfold compared to singular mechanisms, but the current architecture locks in one resource pool, three genome axes, greedy one-step movement, dominant-only encounters, and clonal inheritance. The structural constraint is that no parameter sweep can discover whether multi-resource constraints, interaction diversity, or recombination are necessary because those mechanism classes are not knobs to turn. The research agenda shifts toward designing composable mechanism slots while completing the decomposition wiring that clarifies how much of the founder-grace signal is upstream species-generation versus downstream clade-structuring.
