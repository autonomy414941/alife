# Backlog

- [investigate] [Graded Harvest Base-Preference Inertness] `computeGradedHarvestSecondaryPreference()` currently ignores `basePreference` when `threshold > 0` and `steepness > 0`, making `harvest_secondary_preference` selectively inert under graded harvest. Intervention: make graded harvest preserve and transform the inherited base preference rather than overwrite it with a threshold-only curve, then revalidate the March 26 neutrality claim.
- [expand] [Trait Decoder Bottleneck] `GenomeV2` can store arbitrary trait keys, but the live simulator still routes ecology through hard-coded reads of a small fixed set of loci and taxon caches. Intervention: replace per-operator hard-coded trait access with a metadata-driven trait-expression registry or phenotype decoder.
- [refactor] [Policy Mutation Architecture Drift] Policy mutation, clamping, removability, and distance categorization are still spread across hard-coded trait lists and switch-like branches. Intervention: move locus domain, bounds, mutation kernel, distance category, and removal eligibility into central trait metadata.
- [feat] [Generic Trait Metrics Surface] `StepSummary` and exports still privilege `metabolism`, `harvest`, and `aggression`; generic `GenomeV2` trait distributions and selection summaries are missing. Intervention: add genome-wide trait means, variances, prevalence, and selection-differential reporting to standard summaries and CSV output.
- [validate] [Distance Weight Initialization Opacity] `GenomeV2DistanceWeights` exists, but there is no empirical basis for the default ratios between morphology, bounded policy traits, and unbounded policy thresholds. Intervention: run a bounded calibration panel and set a justified default weighting scheme.
- [validate] [Policy Fitness Attribution Granularity] Policy-fitness bins are still assigned from coarse tick-start context, masking within-tick ecological differences. Intervention: record decision-time context and report context-conditional effect sizes.
- [validate] [Taxonomic Proxy Leakage] Raw species and clade counts can still rise through threshold crossings without ecological distinctness. Intervention: add phenotype-distance-weighted richness, effective niche occupancy, and interaction-distinct diversity metrics.
- [validate] [Matched-Null Fidelity] Relabel-null baselines still ignore founder habitat, crowding, disturbance context, and policy state. Intervention: add stricter matched-null families before treating delta-vs-null gains as evidence.
- [validate] [No Branching Counterfactuals] The study layer cannot fork the same live world into perturbed and unperturbed branches, so mechanism changes are confounded with path dependence. Intervention: add snapshot and replay support for paired counterfactual runs.
- [feat] [Descent Observability] The simulation still discards parent-child edges and phenotype deltas at taxon birth, making innovation reuse and dead ends unmeasurable. Intervention: persist a lightweight genealogy and innovation graph.
- [validate] [Temporal Credit Assignment] Current studies over-index on endpoint and persistence snapshots, making repeated transient innovation hard to score. Intervention: add trajectory metrics such as area under active-clade diversity and innovation survival curves.
- [validate] Run a matched baseline comparing behavioral-control-enabled runs against the memoryless default to measure whether the new policy machinery increases diversification or only adds neutral state.
- [feat] Extend behavioral control to encounters with contingent aggression or retreat policies based on energy state, recent losses, or local context.
- [investigate] [Graded Policy Ecological Neutrality Generalization] After expression fixes, determine whether graded movement, harvest, and reproduction remain neutral under matched protocols or whether neutrality was specific to the prior implementation and homogeneous ecology.
- [expand] [Environmental Complexity] The world is still a static fertility map with shallow disturbance and no persistent biotic construction. Intervention: add long-lived per-cell state layers that agents deposit, erode, and sense.
- [expand] [One-Dimensional Habitat Axis] Habitat remains a single fertility scalar, preventing orthogonal abiotic tradeoffs. Intervention: replace the single field with a vector of abiotic layers and multi-axis tolerance phenotypes.
- [expand] [Resource-Layer Symmetry Ceiling] The two resource pools still share the same fertility and forcing structure, reducing substrate choice to mirrored bookkeeping. Intervention: give each substrate independent spatial and temporal dynamics.
- [expand] [Exogenous Regime Ceiling] Large-scale regimes are still imposed from outside the biota. Intervention: make regeneration, fertility contrast, and disturbance propensity depend on accumulated biotic state or persistent field layers.
- [expand] [Harvest-Spending Policy Coupling Asymmetry] Harvest and spending preferences are heritable but decoupled, so coherent substrate-management strategies cannot evolve. Intervention: let intake and burn policy read each other, energy-pool ratios, or recent intake history.
- [expand] [Observability Blind Spots] Agents still lack direct access to age, disturbance history, encounter outcomes, local taxonomic composition, and seasonal or abiotic signals. Intervention: populate a read-only observation map and expose it to policy logic and analytics.
- [expand] [Memory Horizon] Behavioral control still exposes only `last_harvest_total` as transient memory. Intervention: add multi-tick rolling or decay-weighted memory features for policy inputs.
- [refactor] [Trait Granularity] Habitat preference, trophic level, and defense remain taxon-level summaries rather than per-agent heritable phenotype. Intervention: move ecological phenotype to the agent level and derive taxon summaries from distributions.
- [expand] [Interaction Richness] The interaction alphabet is still fixed to movement, harvest, same-cell theft, reproduction, and thresholded clade founding. Intervention: generalize encounters and foraging into evolvable action channels.
- [expand] [Inheritance Architecture] Reproduction remains single-parent clonal mutation with threshold side effects. Intervention: add multi-parent inheritance or exchange operators and explicit compatibility rules.
- [expand] [Evolutionary Mechanisms] Speciation and cladogenesis still occur in a fully connected torus without barriers or sustained isolation. Intervention: add patch barriers or patch graphs plus lineage-local breeding structure.
- [expand] [Finite Locus Catalog] New loci can only be drawn from a fixed human-authored candidate list. Intervention: replace fixed catalogs with a trait-module factory or parameterized locus generator.
- [expand] [Direct-Encoding Ceiling] Even with more loci, the simulator still lacks an explicit genotype-to-phenotype construction stage. Intervention: add an intermediate phenotype layer with context-dependent expression.
- [validate] [RNG Sequence Non-Reproducibility] Small architectural changes still alter RNG schedules and destroy comparability. Intervention: isolate RNG streams by mechanism or add deterministic checkpoint branching.
