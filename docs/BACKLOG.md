- [critic] [Representational Capacity] Agents only have three mutable genome axes (`metabolism`, `harvest`, `aggression`), while habitat, trophic, and defense traits are derived scalars; no parameter tuning can produce evolvable body plans, sensors, effectors, or additional ecological roles. Intervention: replace the fixed `Genome` schema with extensible heritable loci or modules, and promote habitat / trophic / defense traits from derived summaries to first-class evolvable state.
- [critic] [Interaction Richness] The interaction alphabet is compile-time fixed to movement, harvest, same-cell energy theft, reproduction, and thresholded clade founding, so lineages cannot evolve new interaction types such as signaling, cooperation, public goods, or alternative predation / resource-conversion modes. Intervention: generalize encounters and foraging into evolvable action channels over multiple resource or field layers instead of a single hard-coded transfer equation.
- [critic] [Environmental Complexity] The world is a static fertility map plus one fungible resource pool with exogenous disturbance; agents can deplete and recycle resources but cannot persistently construct, poison, shelter, or otherwise rewrite habitat. Intervention: add persistent per-cell state layers that agents deposit, erode, and sense over many ticks, then feed those layers back into movement, harvest, and survival.
- [critic] [Evolutionary Mechanisms] Speciation and cladogenesis are single-offspring threshold events in a wrapped torus with no barriers, demes, mate choice, or lineage-specific breeding structure, so the system cannot express allopatric isolation, reproductive barriers, or adaptive radiation driven by sustained separation. Intervention: add patch barriers or a patch graph plus lineage-local colonization / breeding pools, and require sustained ecological or spatial separation before promoting a new clade.
- [critic] [Evaluation Blindspots] Current headline metrics focus on active-clade persistence, relabel-null deltas, turnover, and disturbance resilience, which can miss functional innovation, niche construction, and diversification of interaction structure even if they emerge. Intervention: add an open-endedness panel tracking cumulative novel phenotype bins, persistent environment-modification states, lineage interaction-network diversity, and ecological divergence between coexisting clades.

## Critic

- [critic] [Behavioral Control] Agent decisions are memoryless and hard-coded: movement and offspring settlement greedily maximize a one-step local ecology score over five cells, encounters always resolve through the most aggressive occupant, and reproduction is only an energy threshold plus probability gate. No amount of parameter tuning can produce contingent strategies such as dormancy, scouting, pursuit / escape, seasonal migration, retreat-after-loss, or partner choice because agents have no mutable internal state or evolvable controller. Intervention: add per-agent internal state and an evolvable policy layer that maps observations and recent history to movement, harvest, encounter, and reproduction decisions. Dimension: Expressiveness.
- [critic] [Inheritance Architecture] Every descendant is created by single-parent clonal mutation, with speciation and cladogenesis as irreversible threshold side effects of that one lineage. Within this framework the system cannot express recombination, sexual selection, mating compatibility, or horizontal trait transfer, so innovation is limited to local mutation walks around one parent genome. Intervention: introduce multi-parent inheritance or exchange operators plus compatibility / partner-choice rules, and make parentage an explicit part of descendant creation. Dimension: Assumptions.
- [critic] [Descent Observability] `TaxonHistory` retains only per-taxon counts and timelines; the simulation discards parent-child edges, founder trait snapshots beyond a clade founder genome, and phenotype transitions at speciation / cladogenesis time. That makes cumulative novelty, convergence, evolutionary dead ends, and innovation reuse structurally unmeasurable no matter how the Planner reshapes current studies. Intervention: persist a lightweight genealogy / innovation graph with parent ids, founder traits, and phenotype deltas at taxon birth events, then derive novelty and reuse metrics from that graph. Dimension: Feedback Loops.
- [critic] [Temporal Credit Assignment] The study surface mostly aggregates final snapshots, fixed-window turnover, and persistence-threshold deltas, so interventions that create repeated but short-lived innovations or long transient scaffolding before late collapse are nearly invisible to the current optimization loop. Under the present framework the system can optimize endpoint persistence proxies, but not ratcheting search over trajectory quality. Intervention: add first-class trajectory metrics such as area under active-clade diversity, innovation survival curves, regime-switch counts, and recolonization-time distributions alongside the existing final-state summaries. Dimension: Feedback Loops.
