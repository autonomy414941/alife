- [critic] [Representational Capacity] Agents only have three mutable genome axes (`metabolism`, `harvest`, `aggression`), while habitat, trophic, and defense traits are derived scalars; no parameter tuning can produce evolvable body plans, sensors, effectors, or additional ecological roles. Intervention: replace the fixed `Genome` schema with extensible heritable loci or modules, and promote habitat / trophic / defense traits from derived summaries to first-class evolvable state.
- [critic] [Interaction Richness] The interaction alphabet is compile-time fixed to movement, harvest, same-cell energy theft, reproduction, and thresholded clade founding, so lineages cannot evolve new interaction types such as signaling, cooperation, public goods, or alternative predation / resource-conversion modes. Intervention: generalize encounters and foraging into evolvable action channels over multiple resource or field layers instead of a single hard-coded transfer equation.
- [critic] [Environmental Complexity] The world is a static fertility map plus one fungible resource pool with exogenous disturbance; agents can deplete and recycle resources but cannot persistently construct, poison, shelter, or otherwise rewrite habitat. Intervention: add persistent per-cell state layers that agents deposit, erode, and sense over many ticks, then feed those layers back into movement, harvest, and survival.
- [critic] [Evolutionary Mechanisms] Speciation and cladogenesis are single-offspring threshold events in a wrapped torus with no barriers, demes, mate choice, or lineage-specific breeding structure, so the system cannot express allopatric isolation, reproductive barriers, or adaptive radiation driven by sustained separation. Intervention: add patch barriers or a patch graph plus lineage-local colonization / breeding pools, and require sustained ecological or spatial separation before promoting a new clade.
- [critic] [Evaluation Blindspots] Current headline metrics focus on active-clade persistence, relabel-null deltas, turnover, and disturbance resilience, which can miss functional innovation, niche construction, and diversification of interaction structure even if they emerge. Intervention: add an open-endedness panel tracking cumulative novel phenotype bins, persistent environment-modification states, lineage interaction-network diversity, and ecological divergence between coexisting clades.

## Critic

- [critic] [Behavioral Control] Agent decisions are memoryless and hard-coded: movement and offspring settlement greedily maximize a one-step local ecology score over five cells, encounters always resolve through the most aggressive occupant, and reproduction is only an energy threshold plus probability gate. No amount of parameter tuning can produce contingent strategies such as dormancy, scouting, pursuit / escape, seasonal migration, retreat-after-loss, or partner choice because agents have no mutable internal state or evolvable controller. Intervention: add per-agent internal state and an evolvable policy layer that maps observations and recent history to movement, harvest, encounter, and reproduction decisions. Dimension: Expressiveness.
- [critic] [Inheritance Architecture] Every descendant is created by single-parent clonal mutation, with speciation and cladogenesis as irreversible threshold side effects of that one lineage. Within this framework the system cannot express recombination, sexual selection, mating compatibility, or horizontal trait transfer, so innovation is limited to local mutation walks around one parent genome. Intervention: introduce multi-parent inheritance or exchange operators plus compatibility / partner-choice rules, and make parentage an explicit part of descendant creation. Dimension: Assumptions.
- [critic] [Descent Observability] `TaxonHistory` retains only per-taxon counts and timelines; the simulation discards parent-child edges, founder trait snapshots beyond a clade founder genome, and phenotype transitions at speciation / cladogenesis time. That makes cumulative novelty, convergence, evolutionary dead ends, and innovation reuse structurally unmeasurable no matter how the Planner reshapes current studies. Intervention: persist a lightweight genealogy / innovation graph with parent ids, founder traits, and phenotype deltas at taxon birth events, then derive novelty and reuse metrics from that graph. Dimension: Feedback Loops.
- [critic] [Temporal Credit Assignment] The study surface mostly aggregates final snapshots, fixed-window turnover, and persistence-threshold deltas, so interventions that create repeated but short-lived innovations or long transient scaffolding before late collapse are nearly invisible to the current optimization loop. Under the present framework the system can optimize endpoint persistence proxies, but not ratcheting search over trajectory quality. Intervention: add first-class trajectory metrics such as area under active-clade diversity, innovation survival curves, regime-switch counts, and recolonization-time distributions alongside the existing final-state summaries. Dimension: Feedback Loops.
- [critic] [Trait Granularity] Habitat preference, trophic level, and defense level live in taxon-level scalar maps and are only updated on speciation or clade founding, while movement, harvest, and encounters consume those taxon summaries instead of individual phenotype. That makes within-species ecological polymorphism, frequency-dependent morph coexistence, and gradual niche divergence inside an extant species structurally impossible; ecological differentiation only becomes visible after a taxonomic split. Intervention: move ecological phenotype fields to heritable per-agent state updated every birth, and derive species / clade summaries from those distributions instead of using taxon-level lookup tables as the source of truth. Dimension: Expressiveness.
- [critic] [Interaction Topology] `resolveEncounters()` collapses every occupied cell to one aggression-sorted dominant that steals energy from every other occupant with the same transfer rule. Within that architecture the system cannot express pair-specific matchups, non-transitive competition, coalition effects, mutual kills, or context-dependent encounter outcomes because every multispecies contest reduces to a single linear dominance order. Intervention: replace dominant-versus-all resolution with a pairwise encounter scheduler or matchup kernel keyed by participant traits and neighborhood context, and allow multiple outcome types beyond one-way theft. Dimension: Expressiveness.
- [critic] [Dispersal Kinematics] Adults can only stay put or move one cardinal step per tick, and offspring can only settle in the parent cell or its four cardinal neighbors; `dispersalRadius` changes crowding calculations, not move or settlement distance. No amount of parameter tuning can produce rare long-range colonization, dispersal polymorphisms, migration fronts with variable jump lengths, or metapopulation rescue via occasional distant propagules. Intervention: separate sensing radius from movement / settlement kernels, then add configurable or evolvable step-length and offspring-dispersal distributions including rare long-distance events. Dimension: Expressiveness.
- [critic] [Taxonomic Proxy Leakage] The optimization surface still scores success mainly through species / clade counts, persistence windows, turnover, and relabel-null deltas, but taxon identities are minted by threshold crossings rather than by demonstrated ecological distinctness. That means the Planner can win on paper through ecologically near-identical taxonomic splits or by preserving labels without opening new niches, and the current summaries will often treat that as diversification. Intervention: add ecology-weighted diversity metrics that discount near-duplicate taxa, such as phenotype-distance-weighted richness, effective niche occupancy, and interaction-distinct clade counts, and make those co-equal with raw taxon counts in study decisions. Dimension: Feedback Loops.
- [critic] [Occupancy-Only Success Proxy] The canonical species / clade activity probes increment activity once per occupied tick regardless of whether a taxon has one survivor or dominates half the world, so the main objective cannot distinguish barely-alive label persistence from ecologically consequential expansion. Intervention: add abundance-weighted and energy-weighted activity panels plus minimum-viable-population filters, and require interventions to hold up on those metrics alongside the current occupancy-based scores. Dimension: Feedback Loops.
- [critic] [Matched-Null Fidelity] The relabel-null baseline preserves clade birth counts by tick, then randomly reassigns species into pseudo-clades without preserving founder habitat, local crowding, abundance class, or disturbance context. Under that proxy, a stack can look better than null while still failing to create genuinely distinct ecological opportunities. Intervention: add stricter null families that also match founder habitat bin, local occupancy / lineage crowding, abundance class, and disturbance phase at birth before treating delta-vs-null gains as evidence of progress. Dimension: Feedback Loops.
- [critic] [Mechanistic Causality] `TaxonHistory` tracks births, deaths, and population counts, but the system does not retain cause-specific losses, harvest intake, predation transfers, movement costs, or disturbance impacts by species / clade. That makes it structurally impossible to tell whether a reported gain came from better founding, lower same-lineage crowding, reduced predation, or simple resource hoarding. Intervention: add per-taxon demographic and energy ledgers with cause tags, then surface founder-phase and maintenance-phase budgets in the study exports. Dimension: Feedback Loops.
- [critic] [Parameter-Only Search Surface] The current research loop is built around sweeping numeric and boolean fields already present in `SimulationConfig`, with one-off wrappers for each new axis. No amount of Planner optimization inside that surface can discover mechanism classes that are not already encoded as config knobs. Intervention: replace axis-specific sweep wrappers with composable mechanism slots for settlement, encounters, inheritance, and disturbance so future studies can compare whole operator families instead of only retuning existing constants. Dimension: Assumptions.
